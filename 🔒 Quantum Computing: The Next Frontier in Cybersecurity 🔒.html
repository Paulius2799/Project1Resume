What is Quantum Computing?

Traditional computers, the ones we use daily, operate on bits - which can be either a 0 or a 1. Quantum computers, on the other hand, use quantum bits or qubits. The magic of qubits is their ability to be in a state of 0, 1, or both (superposition) simultaneously. This allows quantum computers to process a massive amount of possibilities in a single instant.
